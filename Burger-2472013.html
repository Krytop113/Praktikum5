What Am I?

I am not a sandwich consisting of one or more cooked patties of ground meat, usually beef, placed inside a sliced bun.

Ingredients

  • Burger Bun
  • Beef Patty
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Condiments (Ketchup, Mayo, etc.)

How to Make a Burger

  1. Grill the beef patty.
  2. Toast the burger bun.
  3. Add lettuce, tomato, and cheese.
  4. Place the beef patty inside the bun.
  5. Add condiments and serve!
